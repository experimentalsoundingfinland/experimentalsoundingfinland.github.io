First event of Another Layer concert series. Ebow ring mod spectral drones and ancient chinese song adaptations for 12-string guitar.  
  
Daniel M Karlsson & Lauri Hyvärinen: electric guitars  
Haiyun Yu: 12-string acoustic guitar  
  
Daniel M Karlsson is a long standing fixture of Stockholm’s experimental electronic music scene. He’s been an important part of its key infrastructure including Fylkingen, Norberg Festival and the fabled Elektronmuskstudion EMS where he now teaches various courses.  
As a composer, he works extensively with algorithmic composition to organize sound in a way that brings texture and timbre to the fore.  
  
[https://danielmkarlsson.com/](https://danielmkarlsson.com/)  
  
Lauri Hyvärinen is a Helsinki-based artist working at the intersection of improvisation and electroacoustic composition. His music has been released on labels including Ftarri, NUNC., Dinzu Artefacts and Intonema, and he has performed across Europe, Japan, South Korea and USA at venues and festivals such as Wien Modern, Cafe Oto and Fylkingen.  
  
[https://laurihyvarinen.com/](https://laurihyvarinen.com/)  
  
Haiyun Yu is an artistic researcher and performer from Suzhou, China. Her work explores cultural heritage, ecology, and identity through sound, language, and performance. A Finland Scholarship recipient, she holds a master’s degree from the Sibelius Academy in Arts Management, with studies in Music Technology and Sound Art.
