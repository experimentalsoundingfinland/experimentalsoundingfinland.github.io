HIO’s February concert will be held in Merihaka residents’ association’s kylätupa on 27.2. The program will include free and conducted improvisation. The concerts in kylätupa will be part of Club Rahina’s program. Kylätupa is located on the deck level, opposite to the restaurant Sir Oliver, at Haapaniemenkatu 14. The concert starts at 7pm and entrance is free of charge.
