The Computer Museum of Kallio is a functional computer museum, that brings back the beloved computing experiences from history. The latest achievement is the Atari ST studio, that is up and in operation. Massive amount of hits were composed by using the Atari ST, it was the leading platform for producing music in 1980's and early 1990's. You can test, study and compose by using Atari at the museum.  
  
Check the Atari ST studio in operation:  
[https://youtu.be/BHD3tRXj79s?si=UtePD_9fddbO6YOX](https://youtu.be/BHD3tRXj79s?si=UtePD_9fddbO6YOX)  
  
Further information about the museum on the website:  
[https://www.kalliontietokonemuseo.fi/](https://www.kalliontietokonemuseo.fi/)  
  
There is also Yamaha TG77 Synthesizer, Akai S1000 sampler, Korg M1R and Roland PC-180 keyboard. Cubase is installed on the ST. The Atari studio is build for use.
