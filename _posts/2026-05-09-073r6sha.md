Liput / Tickets 10/5 €  
  
As a part of Ehkä’s Spring Roll Festival, Himera presents a double bill on Saturday the 9th of May with performances by Helsinki based sound artist Heu Hsu and German trumpet player, composer and improviser Axel Dörner. Axel Dörner is one of the most unique voices in free improvisation. Throughout his career, he has developed a completely different language for the trumpet. Most of the ensembles he is part of are characterized by a non-hierarchical collaboration of the musicians involved. Heu Hsu’s practice focuses on site-specific work and the relations between sound, materiality, and cultural landscapes.
