ALERT vol.2 is a five-day micro-festival dedicated to analogue film, live cinema, and experimental performance. Featuring local and international artists working with argentic processes, expanded cinema, and live improvisation. Formulated in 2023 by Helsinki based analogue film artists Marek Waldemar Pluciennik, the festival now returns for its second edition.   [https://shorturl.at/mKXP2](https://shorturl.at/mKXP2)
