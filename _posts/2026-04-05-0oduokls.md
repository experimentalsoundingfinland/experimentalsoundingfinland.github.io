K-L-R trio plays improvised music Easter Sunday evening @Musta Kissa Bar.  
  
J. Koho - electro-electronics, objects, prepared lyre  
V. Lehko - saxophone, clarinet, objects  
J. Rautio - baritone guitar, electronics  
  
In the K-L-R trio's free-playing music, sounds produced in different ways are combined into weaves that find their place in composed structures. Influences from different trends in improvised and experimental music can be heard.  
  
J. Koho approaches sound, sound sources, instruments and playing according to the situation. His work is often inspired by the wonder of sounds and the joy they bring.  
  
Vesa Lehko is interested in exploring the boundaries of music and various sound phenomena through woodwinds, found sounds and objects.  
  
Juha Rautio plays improvised music on the electric guitar and approaches music with a hat of poetry and general avant-gardism in his hand.  
  
The members of the trio have performed at various events and venues focused on experimental music, such as Asbestos Art Space, Kaekuu Festival, Käpylän lippakioski, Kalevan Erikoinen, Äänen Lumo /Toinen Kerros, Pertin Valinta, Rahina-klubi, Pethaus   
and Yö Galleria.
