Rahina club and the Merihaka-seura present Sonic Ambient Merihaka, a sonic environmental event staged in Merihaka's distinctive milieu.    
The audience is led on a tour where they will come across new sonic surprises both on Merihaka's walking deck,  in the park under the linden trees, and also under the walking deck in the huge nicely reverberating parking garage.    
The tour starts at 18:00 in front of Stadia Vocational College Meritalo Unit (Haapaniemenkatu 7-9) and ends with a concert at Merihaan Kylätupa at 19:00-19:30 (Haapaniemenkatu 14).    
The event features sound artists and improvising musicians:    
Katja Lamberg, Ari Lehtinen, Melik Turunen, Kenneth Kovasin, Panu Luukkonen, Mark Reid Bulatovic, Antti Tolvi, Juha Rautio, Jukka-Pekka Kervinen, Jaakko Kuikka, Johanna Vahtola, Vesa Lehko.
