Jonathan Bäckström – double bass   
Adele Sauros – tenor saxophone   
Marcus Wärnheim– alto saxophone   
Benjamin Nylund – drums   
  
Jonathan Bäckström Quartet has become a mainstay in the free jazz community in Finland. The band has toured actively since it was formed in 2020 and has played several sold out concerts in clubs around Finland.   
  
The band’s repertoire primarily consists of original compositions by Bäckström. The music moves somewhere between free jazz and “creative music” with free improvisation as the common denominator. The band released their debut album “JBQ” in 2024.
