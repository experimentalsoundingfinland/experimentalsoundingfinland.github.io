HIO’s first concert of 2026 - and the first of its fourth year of operating - will be traditionally held in Merihaka residents’ association’s kylätupa on 30.1. The program will include free and conducted improvisation. The HIO concerts in kylätupa will be part of Club Rahina’s program. Kylätupa is located on the deck level, opposite to the restaurant Sir Oliver, at Haapaniemenkatu 14. The concert starts at 7pm and entrance is free of charge.
